Our Story

Started in 1976, Ptarmigan Lumber is one of the oldest specialty lumber suppliers in Alberta. We have worked with industry experts on a number landmark projects in the province and are one of a select group of North American importers to carry J-grade dimensional lumber.

Family owned and operated, we strive to deliver high-quality products and exceptional customer service.
